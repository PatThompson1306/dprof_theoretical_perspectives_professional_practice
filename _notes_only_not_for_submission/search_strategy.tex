\subsection{Search Strategy and Database Selection}
\label{subsec:search-strategy}

The search strategy operationalises the four-track integrative framework described in
Section~2.1 through a structured matrix of analytical questions, domain-specific
keywords, and targeted databases. Database selection was determined by the disciplinary
scope of each track, prioritising specialist indices over generalist aggregators where
superior controlled vocabulary and coverage could be demonstrated.
Table~\ref{tab:search-matrix} presents the full search matrix; inclusion and exclusion
criteria are defined in Section~\ref{subsec:criteria}.

\subsection{Inclusion and Exclusion Criteria}
\label{subsec:criteria}

The following criteria were applied consistently across all four tracks to ensure
transparency, reproducibility, and alignment with the systematic integrative design
described in Section~2.1.

\vspace{10pt}

\begin{tcolorbox}[
  enhanced,
  breakable,
  colback=white,
  colframe=incgreen,
  title={\textbf{Inclusion Criteria}},
  fonttitle=\small\bfseries,
  coltitle=white,
  attach boxed title to top left={yshift=-2mm, xshift=6mm},
  boxed title style={colback=incgreen, colframe=incgreen},
  top=6pt, bottom=6pt, left=8pt, right=8pt,
  before upper={\small}
]
\begin{description}[
  leftmargin=1.4cm,
  labelwidth=1.2cm,
  labelsep=0.2cm,
  itemsep=4pt,
  parsep=0pt,
  font=\normalfont\bfseries\small
]
  \item[IC1] Peer-reviewed journal articles, conference proceedings, and edited book
    chapters published 2018 to present.
  \item[IC2] Grey literature from authoritative sources (NPCC, Home Office, CEOP,
    NIST, EU AI Office, ICO) published 2018 to present.
  \item[IC3] Studies addressing NLP, machine learning, or computational linguistics
    applied to safeguarding, criminal justice, or analogous operational contexts.
  \item[IC4] Literature examining grooming, CSA, or online predatory behaviour with
    linguistic, behavioural, or empirical focus.
  \item[IC5] Organisational, cultural, or governance studies relating to technology
    adoption within policing or structurally comparable public sector contexts.
  \item[IC6] English-language sources; non-English sources permitted where a
    peer-reviewed translation or substantive abstract provides sufficient evidential
    grounding with explicit methodological justification.
  \item[IC7] Pre-2018 foundational works where theoretical significance is established
    and no substantively updated equivalent exists, subject to explicit justification
    in the synthesis narrative.
\end{description}
\end{tcolorbox}

\vspace{8pt}

\begin{tcolorbox}[
  enhanced,
  breakable,
  colback=white,
  colframe=excred,
  title={\textbf{Exclusion Criteria}},
  fonttitle=\small\bfseries,
  coltitle=white,
  attach boxed title to top left={yshift=-2mm, xshift=6mm},
  boxed title style={colback=excred, colframe=excred},
  top=6pt, bottom=6pt, left=8pt, right=8pt,
  before upper={\small}
]
\begin{description}[
  leftmargin=1.4cm,
  labelwidth=1.2cm,
  labelsep=0.2cm,
  itemsep=4pt,
  parsep=0pt,
  font=\normalfont\bfseries\small
]
  \item[EC1] Sources published prior to 2018 unless meeting the threshold defined
    in IC7; all exceptions recorded in the review log.
  \item[EC2] Non-peer-reviewed opinion pieces, blog posts, or media articles without
    evidential grounding, unless produced by authoritative professional or statutory
    bodies.
  \item[EC3] Studies focused solely on adult sexual offending with no transferable
    applicability or analytic relevance to child safeguarding contexts.
  \item[EC4] Technical NLP literature without contextual application to safeguarding,
    criminal justice, or operational deployment scenarios.
  \item[EC5] Technology adoption studies from sectors lacking structural or cultural
    comparability to UK policing (e.g.\ private sector commercial contexts without
    analogous accountability frameworks).
  \item[EC6] Duplicate publications; where multiple versions of the same study exist,
    the most recent peer-reviewed version is retained and earlier versions excluded.
  \item[EC7] Sources for which full text cannot be obtained through institutional
    access, inter-library loan, or open-access repositories within a reasonable
    retrieval window.
\end{description}
\end{tcolorbox}
